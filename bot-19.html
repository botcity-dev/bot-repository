Wordle Bot
by Alex Sander on 15/02/2022
This is a bot that plays the game Wordle, albeit in its alternative version called Hello Wordle. This version allows you to play as many times a day as you want, and to change the word size to a number between 4 and 11. As a demonstration, this bot plays once for every word size, starting at 11.
