About

This is a community database of Bot automation codes.

Whether you use BotCity, Selenium, Puppeteer, RobotFramework, UiPath or any other framework or tools, here is the place for you to showcase your automation and share it with the world.

These bots are developed by the community. If you have questions about the projects or need support, please contact the automation authors directly.

Contributing a Code

Contributing to the BotRepository is super easy and takes less than two minutes.
Submit the basic information needed and our team will add your contribution to the Bot Repository.
Click here to get started.

This website as well as the list of bots displayed here are being version controlled using GitHub on the botcity-dev/bot-repository repository.
